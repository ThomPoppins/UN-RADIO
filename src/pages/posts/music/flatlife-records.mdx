<section>
  <h2>About</h2>
  <p>
    Acid techno / techno label based in the Netherlands, owned and run by{" "}
    <a href="https://www.discogs.com/artist/1322665-J-de-Koeijer">
      J. de Koeijer
    </a>{" "}
    and started officially January 1st, 2009 in Eindhoven to promote the music
    of <a href="https://www.discogs.com/artist/1322663-Jack-Wax">Jack Wax</a>.
    Before 2009; some CD releases were released without publishing or rights
    society's by{" "}
    <a href="https://www.discogs.com/artist/121352-DJ-Jape-du-Marie">
      DJ Jape du Marie
    </a>{" "}
    & <a href="https://www.discogs.com/artist/7653679-Holik-2">Holik (2)</a>.
    These releases are very rare and only spread amongst friends & family from{" "}
    <a href="https://www.discogs.com/artist/1322665-J-de-Koeijer">
      J. de Koeijer
    </a>
    . Most releases after 2009 are distributed worldwide by <a href="https://www.discogs.com/label/273199-Triple-Vision">
      Triple Vision
    </a>. Flatlife Records became in 2015 also an official vinyl-distributor for
    existing labels but also for new labels (in close cooperation with Triple Vision).
  </p>
</section>
