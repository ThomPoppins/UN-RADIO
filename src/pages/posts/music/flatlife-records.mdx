Acid techno / techno label based in the Netherlands, owned and run by [J. de Koeijer](https://www.discogs.com/artist/1322665-J-de-Koeijer) and started officially January 1st, 2009 in Eindhoven to promote the music of [Jack Wax](https://www.discogs.com/artist/1322663-Jack-Wax).  
Before 2009; some CD releases were released without publishing or rights society's by [DJ Jape du Marie](https://www.discogs.com/artist/121352-DJ-Jape-du-Marie) & [Holik (2)](https://www.discogs.com/artist/7653679-Holik-2). These releases are very rare and only spread amongst friends & family from [J. de Koeijer](https://www.discogs.com/artist/1322665-J-de-Koeijer). Most releases after 2009 are distributed worldwide by [Triple Vision](https://www.discogs.com/label/273199-Triple-Vision). Flatlife Records became in 2015 also an official vinyl-distributor for existing labels but also for new labels (in close cooperation with Triple Vision).
