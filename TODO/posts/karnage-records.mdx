## About

<img src="" />

[Karnage Records](https://karnagerecords.bandcamp.com/) is an independent Hardcore label established in 1998 by DJ Thanos, born in Toulouse, France. The label has crafted a distinctive universe encompassing Hardcore, industrial, speedcore, and more.

The journey began with [Karnage 01] produced by Mouse, followed by Alcore and Radium with [Karnage 02] and [Karnage 03]. After securing our initial significant hits, we ventured into the exciting realm of organizing events.

Over the past 20 years, Karnage Records has released numerous tracks and hosted over 200 events worldwide, driven by an unwavering passion for hardcore against all odds.

Our catalog primarily focuses on Hardcore & Industrial. Continuously reinventing ourselves, we traverse styles at the core of musical renewal.
